  • Easy installation and cloud-based storage.
  • Advanced features like motion detection, AI recognition, and night vision.
  • Integration with smart home platforms like Alexa and Google Assistant.

  • Tiandy

  • Tiandy is a professional surveillance brand known for its AI-driven video solutions. It's popular in enterprise-level applications.

  • Key Features

  • Advanced AI technology, including facial recognition and behavior analytics.
  • Starlight technology for low-light performance.
  • Wide range of PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom) cameras.

  • Popular Use: Public surveillance, industrial, and large-scale security systems.

  • Anten

  • Anten is less globally recognized but offers reliable and budget-friendly security cameras and solutions.

  • Key Features

  • Basic surveillance cameras with essential features like motion detection and night vision.
  • Affordable options for small-scale security setups.

  • Popular Use: Budget-friendly residential and small business applications.

  • HIKVision

  • HIKVision is one of the world's largest suppliers of video surveillance products and solutions. They cater to both residential and enterprise needs.

  • Key Features

  • Comprehensive product range including IP cameras, NVRs, and AI-based systems.
  • Technologies like DarkFighter for ultra-low-light performance and AcuSense for AI-based alerts.
  • Integration with large-scale IoT ecosystems.

  • Popular Use: Residential, commercial, government, and industrial security.
